How it works

From statement drop to a signed-off reconciliation
The pipelineeach statement flows left → right, untouched by hand
1

Drop

Watched folder · email · upload

A bank statement PDF arrives — emailed to a monitored mailbox, dropped in a folder, or uploaded from the overview.

2

OCR & extract

Paperless-ngx

Paperless OCRs the document and a post-consume hook extracts the balance, account number, and statement date.

3

Match

n8n workflow

A workflow pulls the GL balance for that account, compares it to the statement within tolerance, and computes the variance.

4

Record

Reconciliation DB

The verdict — reconciled, variance, or unmatched — is written back with the OCR evidence and a document link.

5

Review

Workspace

Exceptions surface on the board for a preparer to resolve, an approver to sign off, and a director to oversee.
