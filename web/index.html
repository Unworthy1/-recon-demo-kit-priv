One board, from drop to sign-off

Every account in the close, its ledger balance, the OCR'd statement balance, the variance, and who owns it — with the exceptions pulled to the top.

Drop-and-go ingestion

Email it, drop it in a watched folder, or upload it. Paperless OCRs the statement and extracts the balance, account number, and date automatically.

Workflow-driven matching

A reconciliation workflow pulls the GL balance, compares it to the statement within tolerance, and writes the verdict back — reconciled, variance, or unmatched.

Exception-first review

Filter to just your variances. Attach supporting docs, leave a resolution note, and mark resolved — every action stamped with who and when.

Roles & approvals

Preparers (junior / senior) prepare, Principals approve, Directors review. Each item carries a work status as it moves through the close.

Director visibility

A productivity window into the whole team — throughput per preparer, status mix, and aging of open exceptions across the close.

Drops into the enterprise

The same shape maps to whatever you run — your documents (Laserfiche, SharePoint…) and your ledger (Microsoft Dynamics 365, NetSuite, SAP…). The demo just swaps in open-source stand-ins.

Works with your stack

Reconciliation doesn't have to live inside any one ERP. Pluggable, read-only adapters connect to the systems you already run — reference adapters for the common ones, and a generic ODBC / CSV / API path for everything else. The board, workflow, and audit trail are identical no matter what feeds them.
